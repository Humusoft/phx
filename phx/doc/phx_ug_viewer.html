Interactive Viewer

A ready-made 3-D window with a sky, camera and mouse editing

Instead of bare axes you can use the enhanced viewer phx.extra.Viewer with a sky, a movable camera and the ability to edit the scene with the mouse:

[~, ax] = phx.extra.Viewer("clear", "ViewMode", "sky");
% then add bodies into the ax axes as usual
phx.Body(ax, "Position", [0 0 2], "Shape", {"Sphere"});
ControlAction
Left mouse buttonPan the camera.
Middle buttonOrbit the camera.
WheelZoom; with Shift, the field of view.
Arrows / WASDPan and move the camera.
Double-clickSelect / activate a body (edit mode).
F1 … F5, HomeHelp, light, view mode, free run, reset.
See also phx.extra.Viewer
